Introduction

Project Name

Sports Partner

Why we do this

Peopel are paying more and more attention to their health.

Some people want to start exercising. However, with no experience, they do not know where to start.

Some people execrise occasionally. Without partner, they can not keep doing this.

Some people are really good at this. However, they feel a bit lonely while they do not have a partner

So we decide to do this:

Match up the guys who want to execrise

For people who just start, they can find a partner to help them.

For people who can to keep doing this, they can have a partner to encourage one another.

For people who really special at it, they can have a friend, or even make some money.

How it works

First, people who want a partner need to register on the website.

They need to fill a form about their basic information, and what they like to do. Eg. Basketball, Football or something else.

Then their information will be stored in the database.

And after they register successfully, then they can choose the conditions about the matching.

For example, if they want to find a partner to run, they can choose to match people who are 5 km around and want to have a partner to run. Or 10 km around.

Then there will be a list of people for they to choose, and send message to ask whether they want to run at a specific time.

This is the basic information about how it works.
